Features

Our system supports various hand gestures to control your computer effortlessly.

Cursor Movement

Move your hand to control the cursor smoothly.

Left Click

Use thumb & index finger gestures to perform different clicks.

Right Click

Use thumb & middle finger gestures to perform different clicks.

Double Click

Use thumb & ring finger gestures to perform different clicks.

Scrolling up

Scroll up and down by raising your pinky.

Scrolling down

Scroll up and down by raising your index finger and thumb.

Zoom in

Zoom in by raising and opening your index and middle fingers.

Zoom out

Zoom out by raising and closing your index and middle fingers.

Drag & Drop

Hold all fingers down to drag items, lift to drop.
